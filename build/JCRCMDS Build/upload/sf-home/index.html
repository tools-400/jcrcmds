 Version: 1.0.4.b001

Introduction

JCRCMDS is an open source plug-in for RDi 9.5.1.3+. It is a wrapper plug-in for the JCRCMDS utility of Craig Rutledge. The JCRCMDS utility converts fixed format H, F and D specifications into free format DCL-* statements.

JCRCMDS features are driven from our ideas and needs, but everybody is encouraged to contribute suggestions and manpower to improve the power of JCRCMDS.
